Welcome to Catering Services Zimbabwe

CEE'S KITCHEN catering Services Zimbabwe was established in 2021, specializing in
Corporate and Industrial Catering, as well as outdoor catering
events (funerals, memorial services, parties etc)
Our motto “TINOGONESA”, speaks for itself.

Traditional Meals

Traditional meals feature white sadza, zviyo sadza, mhunga sadza, peanut butter rice, green leaf vegetables, and various meats like beef, chicken, muzondo, guru, matumbu, beef bones, fish, gango, and goat meat.
Western Meals

Western meals include rice, pasta potatoes for starch. Chicken, fish, beef, pork for protein and a variety of vegetables.
Vegetarian Meals

For our Vegetarian customers we have a choice of bean stews, mushroom dishes, egg dishes and soya dishes.

Regulatory requirements
CEE'S KITCHEN catering Services Zimbabwe fulfills all employment regulatory criteria. Our entity is affiliated with the National Employment Council
for the Catering Industry (NNECCI), National Social Services Association (NSSA), Pension Fund, and PRAZ. Each employee is likewise
enrolled with NECCI and NSSA, with contributions submitted monthly.

Our team members likewise receive yearly medical examinations, with a medical certificate provided for every individual.
